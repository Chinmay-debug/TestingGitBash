Take A Brief Look
of Virendra Sehwag's Life

  • 20 OCTOBER 1978 VIRENDRA SEHWAG WAS BORN.
  • 1999 HE PLAYED HIS FIRST ONE DAY INTERNATIONAL.
  • 2001 HE JOINED THE INDIAN TEST SIDE. AND PLAYED MATCH AGAINST SOUTH AFRICA.
  • 2006 HE PLAYED T20I AGAINST SOUTH AFRICA.
  • 2008 SUBSEQUENTLY BECOMED THE FIRST PLAYER OF ANY NATIONALITY TO RETAIN AWARD FOR 2009.
  • 2009 HE BECOMED FIRST INDIAN TO BE HONOURED AS THE WIDSEN LEADING CRICKETER IN THE WORLD.
  • 2 OCTOBER 2012 HE PLAYED HIS LAST T20I AGAINST SOUTH AFRICA.
  • 3 JANUARY 2013 HE PLAYED HIS LAST ODI AGAINST PAKISTAN.
  • 2 MARCH 2013 HE PLAYED HIS LAST TEST AGAINST AUSTRALIA.
  • 2013 HE RETIRED FROM INTERNATIONAL SIDE.
